Eric Ries introduces a systematic approach to launching products under uncertainty. Our review highlights its focus on testing assumptions quickly to minimize wasted effort.

## Why we recommend it

The Build-Measure-Learn cycle encourages continuous improvement based on real customer feedback. This approach reduces risk compared to traditional long development cycles.

## Key takeaways

1. **Validated learning guides progress** — Data beats assumptions.
2. **Minimum Viable Products reduce risk** — Launch early to test ideas.
3. **Adaptability determines survival** — Flexibility enables growth.

## Best for

Entrepreneurs, product managers, and innovators building new ventures. Particularly useful for technology startups.
