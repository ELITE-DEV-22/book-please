*Hooked* outlines a framework for creating products that users return to repeatedly. Our review finds it particularly relevant for app developers and entrepreneurs.

## Why we recommend it

The Hook Model—Trigger, Action, Variable Reward, Investment—explains how digital platforms cultivate engagement. Ethical considerations are also discussed, encouraging responsible design.

## Key takeaways

1. **Behavioral psychology drives product success** — Understanding users is crucial.
2. **Variable rewards sustain engagement** — Uncertainty increases interest.
3. **User investment builds loyalty** — Personalization strengthens attachment.

## Best for

Product managers, UX designers, and entrepreneurs building digital platforms.
